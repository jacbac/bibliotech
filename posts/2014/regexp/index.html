 Is ISBN 10

Description: Validation of 10 digit ISBN. The ISBN number must be preceded by the text “ISBN:” or “ISBN-10:” , the colon is optional. Regular Expression: ISBN(?:-10)?:?\x20(?=.{13}$)\d{1,5}([- ])\d{1,7}\1\d{1,6}\1(\d|X)$ Pass: ISBN-10: 0-93028-923-4 Fail: ISBN-13: 978-0-5960-0289-3

Is ISBN 13

Description: Validation of new 13 digit ISBN. The ISBN number must be preceded by the text “ISBN:” or “ISBN-13:” , the colon is optional. Regular Expression: ISBN(?:-13)?:?\x20*(?=.{17}$)97(?:8|9)([ -])\d{1,5}\1\d{1,7}\1\d{1,6}\1\d$ Pass: ISBN-13: 978-0-5960-0289-3 Fail: ISBN-10: 0596002890
